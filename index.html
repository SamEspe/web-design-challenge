Weather Analysis Project


What is this Project?

The goal of this project was see how different weather measurements relate to the latitude of the measured location. To do this, we used data obtained from OpenWeatherAPI on a list of over 500 cities around the world. From this data, I extracted some key measurements to compare: temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.


To examine the relationship between the measurements and the city's latitude, I graphed the measurement values against the city's latitude. The only measurement that had a clear relationship was the temperature.
