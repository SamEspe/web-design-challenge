Wind Speed and Latitude

This plot compares the wind speed in cities to their latitude. There doesn't seem to be much of a trend here. There is a broad grouping of data points between 0 and 15 miles per hour. These are distributed across the latitude range, and they are wind speeds that one might expect on a typical day in most locales.
